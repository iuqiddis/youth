--- layout: post title: On T.A.'s date: '2006-03-20T17:06:00.000-08:00' author: Iuqiddis tags: - School - Berkeley modified_time: '2011-06-18T01:30:03.274-07:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-17098359.post-114290430225776149 blogger_orig_url: http://iuqiddis.blogspot.com/2006/03/on-t.html --- Like most other science classes, my physics class has a lab and discussion section to it. Just like any other class, each discussion section has their own T.A.. However, the labs are done in conjunction with another discussion section so basically there are two classes (each with their own T.A.) doing a particular lab together.

Today I go to lab as usual, and start the lab. As we're doing the lab we come across a totally new concept, so the other section's T.A. goes on the board and introduces the concepts to all the students (both sections).

I figure I should pay attention so I look up. And then I just start staring at her. She was rather hot. Well she wasn't really hot. She was probably a seven to eight. But I didn't have my glasses on, so she became a definite eight. Then I had to factor in the fact that we're in berkeley, and she's a physics T.A.; obviously she becomes a 9. So i continue staring at her while she talks.

After a while, I'm like dude shame on you, so I turn aside and look at my work. But then I realize I don't know the concepts yet, so I look up to pay attention to the instructions she's giving. And then I start staring at her again.

After about half an hour, the class ended.
I payed attention to her yet didn't pay any attention to her.
I still don't know how to do the worksheets.

Next time, I think my section's T.A. should present the concepts. She doesn't distract me when I look at her :P
