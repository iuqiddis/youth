A Proof

18 Nov 2005 Something cruel and funny from Han.


A Mathematical Proof that Rakesh will not get an A in his Chem class:

First let me establish a fundamental.

Lemma 1: Rakesh is dumb.
Proof: Look at his high school transcript.

Theorem 1.1: Rakesh cannot get an A in chem.

Proof:
First, let us suppose he did get an A in chem. Then we must come to the conclusion that he
is of reasonable intelligence. However, this is contradictory to the assertion of Lemma 1 which states that Rakesh is dumb. Thus through proof by contradiction, we conclude that Rakesh cannot get an A in chem.
