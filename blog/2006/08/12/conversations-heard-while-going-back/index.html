Conversations Heard While Going Back

12 Aug 2006 Conversations heard while going back to BART after my class

Two kids with skateboards are walking ahead of me. They are near a metal globe --about a meter in diameter--that's located near the campus entrance 6.

Dude 1: Dude, I bet I can climb over that fucking circle.
Dude 2: Dude you can't, cause its a fucking sphere

Walking on Shattuck towards BART. There is a woman with two babies in a stroller coming towards me. In front of me are two college kids (going my direction)

Guy 1: (To his friend) Cute kids.
Guy 2: Cute Mom.
